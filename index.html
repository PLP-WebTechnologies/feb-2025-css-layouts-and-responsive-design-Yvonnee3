About Task Interiors

We are an Interior Design company founded in the year 2004 by Yvonne Mueni.Our mission is to not only create interiors aesthetically pleasing but also functional,sustainable and perfectly tailored to each client's unique needs and lifestlye.We achieve this by closely working with our clients,listening attentively to their vision and translating it into a design that exceeds their expectations.

Inspired by the vibrant landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Kenya,our designs often incorporate natural materials and locally sourced elements,creating spaces that feel both contemporary and connected to their environment.
